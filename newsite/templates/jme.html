J. Morris “Morrie” Evans was a member of Haverford College’s class of 1943. He has contributed significantly to The Cope Evans Collection. The collection is comprised primarily of letters but also includes diaries and financial documents. A recently approved addition donated by Evans added several shipping documents which illuminate some of the Cope Evans business patterns. This new batch of documents includes letters received and sent by Evans himself along with an appraisal of the collection by Carmen Valentino. The affidavit is shown below.

Evans was a conscientious objector during World War II. He worked on farms as alternative service. After the war, he began work as an accountant at the Philadelphia Quartz company which later became the PQ corporation. J. Morris Evans was subsequently Vice-President and and Treasurer of the Corporation.
